Our Process

ASSESS

Conduct an assessment of your organization to determine your baseline and gather community feedback to determine areas for improvement. This includes learning about previous equity work your company has undertaken, your mission/vision/ and values, and a community pulse survey to determine baseline knowledge and engagement of staff, clients, or leadership

PLAN

Come up with SMARTIE goals for your organization. What changes do you want to see? How are you striving to incorporate equity within your business? Development of a community informed and data driven action plan to reach your goals. What physical and intellectual steps can be taken to reach your goals?

ACTION

Implementation of data informed activities following change management principles.
