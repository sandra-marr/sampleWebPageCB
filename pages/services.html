OUR PROCESS

ASSESS

Conduct a business assessment and community pulse survey to gather baseline data within your organization. This includes understanding previous equity work your company has undertaken, your mission/vision/ and values, and employee or leadership knowledge of gender equity.

PLAN

Develop specific, measurable, inclusive, equitable, and attainable goals for your organization. We may ask:

  • What changes do you want to see?
  • What spurred your interest in equity consultation at this time?
  • How are you striving to incorporate equity within your business?
  • What physical and intellectual steps can be taken to reach your goals?

ACTION

Implement activities based on data collected during the assessment phase. Action will be trauma informed and follow change management principles.
