MISSION

PLUS* Equity Consulting provides Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion consultation to foster more inclusive and equitable work environments for transgender and nonbinary employees, clients, and communities. We do this through an array of services that are data driven and adaptable to the DEI needs of your organization.

MEET THE TEAM

Caitlyn Brooks (She/they)

Caitlyn comes to this work through her own gender journey and as a member of the LGBTQ+ community. She aspires to use her technical skills and formal education to elevate the voices of other members of the LGBTQ+ community. Originally from the East Coast, she has the lived experience of diverse perspectives to lead individuals in all stages of their equity journey with empathy. Caitlyn seeks respite in the endless nature that the PNW offers and lives with their partner Z and their dog Scooter. She is deeply invested in working with employers to reduce the trauma experienced by transgender and nonbinary individuals.
