OUR MISSION

PLUS* Equity Consulting provides Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion consultation to foster more inclusive and equitable work environments for transgender and nonbinary employees, clients, and communities. We do this through an array of services that are data driven and adaptable to the DEI needs of your organization.

MEET THE TEAM

Caitlyn Brooks (She/They)

Caitlyn comes to this work through her own gender journey and as a member of the LGBTQ+ community. She aspires to use her technical skills and formal education to elevate the voices of LGBTQ+ individuals. Originally from the East Coast, Caitlyn has the lived experience of diverse perspectives to lead others in all stages of their equity journey with empathy. Caitlyn is deeply invested in working with employers to reduce the trauma experienced by transgender and nonbinary individuals in the workplace. Caitlyn seeks respite in the endless nature that the PNW offers and lives with their partner and their dog Scooter.

Grace Mussa (She/Her)

Grace is a licensed clinical social worker with extensive experience in crisis de-escalation, empathetic active listening, and equitable facilitation practices. As a first generation, biracial mental health counselor, Grace brings a depth of lived experience to her practice. Grace's skills provide an emotionally safe environment to discuss difficult topics. Grace is a greater Seattle native and loves everything mountainous, oceanic, adventurous, and calming. You can find Grace curled up with a book and her fluffy cat when not doing the damn thing.

Jared Keirn (Any pronoun said with respect)

Jared's life learned skills come from their varied experiences, including working as an epidemiologist, supporting data collection best practices during the 2020 Census, supervising Washington's Crisis/Life Line, and serving in the US Navy. While Jared has gathered a wide array of technical skills in these positions, having to navigate different institutions as a queer, mixed race person has blossomed invaluable social skills that help foster a connection with many different perspectives. When not securing the bag, you may find Jared skiing, turning a lewk, or twirling with their partner.
