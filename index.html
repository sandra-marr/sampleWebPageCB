Our Mission

We provide consultation to businesses, nonprofits, and government organizations to create Transgender and Nonbinary inclusive spaces, establish gender equity in your business, and proactively engage members of the LGBTQ+ community. Our work is commited to providing data driven solutions that center the experience of transgender and nonbinary individuals.
Equity is the foundation of everything we do and we belive that by creating inclusive spaces for LGBTQIA+ individuals everyone will benefit.
